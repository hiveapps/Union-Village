Company Timeline

  • FIVE GUYS ON A COUCH

    January 2010

    The co-founders sat on a couch at Baker’s home and talked about a hospital and senior retirement community, supported by the Unions, in Las Vegas.

  • THE INTEGRATION PARADIGM

    February 9, 2010

    The co-founders first conceived the Integrated Health Village.

  • FINDING THE SITE

    June, 2010

    After an extensive Site Study of 11 possible locations in Clark County, Nevada, the Henderson/Galleria site was selected for the design development and construction of the first Integrated Health Village.

  • CAPITAL RAISE

    August, 2010

    Union Village LLC circulated its first offering to raise capital.

  • PRESS RELEASE

    April 7, 2011

    The first press announcement for UnionVillage.

  • APPROVAL BY CITY OF HENDERSON

    June, 2011

    Henderson City Council formally approved the sale of the property to UnionVillage, LLC.

  • HOSPITAL PARTNER

    June, 2013

    Universal Health Services signed a LOI to purchase 35 acres in UnionVillage to build a state-of-the-art hospital.

  • GROUND BREAKING

    October 8, 2014

    Henderson Hospital Breaks Ground.

  • MORE PARTNERS

    January, 2015

    UnionVillage signs LOI’s with a number of users including: Skilled Nursing, Long Term Acute Care, Senior Living, Apartment Developer, Dialysis Clinic, Wellness Clinic and Retail development.
