Senior Apartments

Beautiful, vibrant and affordable senior living for those 55+ is coming to Union Village. The project will be comprised of three-story mid-rise buildings and carriage style homes, some with garages.

These brand new and spacious floor plans will feature quality interior finishes including Energy Star-rated Clean Steel kitchen appliances, granite countertops and in-home washer/dryer option.

Community amenities will consist of Wi-Fi care, arts-and-craft room and ability to enjoy free membership in the fitness center and spa at Union Village.
