Emerald Village

Union Village’s golden-age residents who require some measure of daily support or specialized health care are known as Emerald Villagers. For these residents, Emerald Village homes are provided, offering beautiful floorplans, comfortable, familiar décor in private bedrooms, and kitchens, living, and family rooms shared with other Emerald Villagers. These homes are designed to be well-suited to the lifestyle and progressive needs of home-based care that would traditionally be provided in “institutional” environments such as skilled nursing, long-term acute, and Alzheimer/memory care facilities. Emerald Villagers can also enjoy exclusive access to the many amenities within Emerald Village, accompanied by family, friends, and as needed, their resident caregivers.

Center for Sports, Health & Healing

The Center for Sports, Health and Healing at Union Village can be enjoyed by Union Village residents, other members of the surrounding communities, and professional athletes seeking training and rehabilitation. The Valley’s finest providers will offer myriad specialized services and integrative care at the Center’s “Power-Aging Wellness Center” for golden-age clientele, and at the “Training and Rehabilitation Center” for professional athletes. All providers’ share the goal to provide for the benefit of healing and bringing the human condition into balance, and promoting a vital, quality lifestyle that preserves this balance.
