The Integrated Health Village is a place where the traditional providers (Inpatient Care, Outpatient Care, Physicians, Skilled Nursing, Home Health, Senior Living, and Wellness) have merged to establish a complete continuum of care.

The Integrated Health Village master concept enables these providers ("Village Partners") to align philosophically and collaborate on the delivery of comprehensive, quality healthcare to their patients ("Villagers").

The Village Integration concept includes a suite of solutions, including the provision of a common leading edge infrastructures of shared medical technology and record management services.

The Integrated Health Village at Henderson, Nevada is indeed the future of healthcare, bringing medical innovation to this and other integrated campuses around the world where people can; work and play, heal and hope, live and love, and learn and grow.
