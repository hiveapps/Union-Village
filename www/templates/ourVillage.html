Imagine walking into one of the first and largest integrated villages in the world. Union Village is creating a vibrant, architecturally aesthetic, technologically innovative, and environmentally sustainable master-planned village offering a promenade of specialty retail and entertainment, restaurants, residential, and a world-class healthcare complex.

As you approach the Union Village Promenade, you will find a variety of specialty retail shops and restaurants.

At one end of the Promenade stands “all suite” hotel accommodations.

Along the Promenade you will discover myriad sources of entertainment and technology, from eco-friendly waterscapes, interactive experiences, and social and musical venues.

As you explore Union Village, you will discover reality in the making with a world-class health care and wellness complex, including the anchor Henderson Hospital, one of the most successful start-ups in the U.S., an array of Western and Eastern treatment modalities, dialysis clinic, golden-age residential options, and long-term-acute and skilled-nursing care.

The Union Village vision and innovation is further revealed with The Center for Sports, Health and Healing, offering integrative approaches and a continuum of care to promote, preserve and restore strength, health and vitality. Holistic, mind-body and bio-identical therapies, personalized fitness and nutrition programs, professional athletic and rehabilitation training, a full-service spa, and myriad other health-inspiring and therapeutic services will be available.
