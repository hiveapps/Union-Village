Coming From Las Vegas, NV

  1. Head northeast on N Las Vegas Blvd toward E Ogden Ave
  2. Turn right to merge onto I-515 S/US-93 S/US-95 S
  3. Take exit 64B for Galleria Drive
  4. Keep left at the fork to continue toward Galleria Dr
  5. Use any lane to turn left onto Galleria Dr
  6. Make a U-turn at N Gibson Rd
  7. Destination will be on the right, 1050 Galleria Dr Henderson, NV 89011
